Transportation Services

Our company delivers ordered equipment to any location around the globe using the most convenient method for you—by air, sea, or road transport. We form the most efficient logistics chains through our extensive network of partners.

Our specialists carefully select the appropriate transport for each order, conduct detailed tariff calculations, and develop optimal routes considering weather conditions and road conditions. This approach significantly reduces time delays. With complete control over the transportation process, our staff can promptly make route adjustments at any stage of delivery.

Customs Clearance Assistance

Our company will handle all the necessary customs documentation and assist in calculating customs duties and other fees.

Customs clearance is a complex process that requires a serious approach, professionalism, and consultation at every stage.

    We offer consulting support throughout all stages of customs clearance:

  • Collection of necessary documents for transportation and preparation of the customs declaration.
  • Selection of appropriate HS codes.
  • Calculation of customs duties and advice on minimizing customs payments.
  • Preparation of the complete set of transport and cargo documents, as well as additional information about the goods.
